# BTBT App

## Introduction
A social media platform for beer enthusiast to share their favourite moments with their favourite drink. After users create an account with the platform, they will be able to interact with other users of the app. App users will be able to create and upload content as well as interact with other users' content, such as leaving comments and saving contents to their Favourites tab.
